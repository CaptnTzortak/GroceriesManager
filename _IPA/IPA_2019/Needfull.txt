layout_width & layout_height

The layout_width and layout_height properties are both set to match_parent. 
The ConstraintLayout is the root view, so the "parent" layout size is effectively the size of the screen on the device. 
The ConstraintLayout will be the same width and height as the screen.
Tip: All views must have layout_width and layout_height properties.
